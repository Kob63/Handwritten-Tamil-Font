FONTLOG for MyHands

This file provides information about the MyHands font.

Font Family: MyHands  
Designer: Oldman  
License: SIL Open Font License v1.1

Description:  
MyHands is a personal handwritten Tamil font inspired by the natural penmanship of an old farmer. Created entirely using a mouse, it captures a rustic, human feel — perfect for casual writing, storytelling, education, and cultural projects.

Acknowledgments:  
Created with care by Oldman. Special thanks to those who keep our native scripts and stories alive.
